Welcome to GRC Races!

The Grandview Running Club meets locally in the locale of Grandview Heights, Ohio, every Tuesday evening to run at 7:00pm (we've never missed a week!). We pick a different bar to meet and drink at every week with runs usually around 4-5 miles.

This website was started by a member of the group as a way to organize the members' races. What more needs to be said?

To find out more about GRC, visit our Meetup group and like us on Facebook.

Feel free to register and join us!
